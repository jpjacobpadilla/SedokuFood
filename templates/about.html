Overarching Goal

We aim to provide our users with a personalized schedule of dishes which meets their budgeting goals. This includes consistently updating user preferences, and improving our recommender system.

Key Features

To obtain the 3 million data points, our team scraped data from menupages.com. This provided us with dishes from over 4,000 restaurants in New York City. Additionally, we utilized Yelp’s API to futher refine our restaurant’s information.

Data Sources

In addition to providing users with a personalized weekly schedule, our platform also consistently updates users’ preferences every week through the food rating feature. This provides us with data which allows the recommender system to better cluster restaurants and make more accurate predictions on user preferences.

Motivation Behind Project

As college students, we found ourselves overspending on food. From frequently ordering in, to frequenting too many restaurants every weekend, we needed help. However, there was no platform which merged, and prioritized, budgeting needs and user preferences. Thus, we decided to make Sedoku ourselves.
